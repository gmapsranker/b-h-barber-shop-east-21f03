Frequently Asked Questions About Kids Hair Cut

How much do kids' haircuts cost and what ages do you serve?
Our kids' haircuts are $30 for children ages 12 and under. For toddlers (ages 1-3), we charge $35 since these cuts often require extra time and attention. We welcome children of all ages and have special booster seats for our youngest clients. First-time haircuts receive a complimentary 'First Haircut Certificate' and a photo if desired.
Do I need an appointment for my child's haircut? How long does it usually take?
While we do accept walk-ins, we strongly recommend appointments for children's haircuts, especially during after-school hours (3-6pm) and weekends. A typical kids' cut takes 20-30 minutes, but we schedule 45-minute slots to ensure we have plenty of time to work with more active children. Book appointments through our website or by calling (212) 555-0123.
What do you do to make children feel comfortable during their haircut?
We have dedicated barbers who specialize in children's cuts and know how to keep kids calm and entertained. Our chairs have built-in tablet holders with kid-friendly videos, and we provide lollipops (with parent's permission). We also have a small toy chest for after the cut and use special cape designs featuring popular cartoon characters. For nervous first-timers, we're happy to do a quick salon tour before starting.
Are your barbers licensed and experienced with children's haircuts?
All our barbers are NYS licensed and have undergone additional training specifically for children's haircuts. Our team has a combined 25+ years of experience cutting kids' hair, and we regularly attend workshops to stay current with the latest techniques and trends in children's hairstyling. We're also trained in handling sensory-sensitive children and those with special needs.
What if my child is unhappy with their haircut?
We stand behind our work and want every child to leave happy. If you're not satisfied with the cut, please let us know within 48 hours, and we'll gladly make adjustments free of charge. We take detailed notes on each child's preferences and cutting techniques that work best for them, ensuring consistency with future visits. For first-time clients, we recommend bringing a photo of the desired style to ensure we meet your expectations.
