Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Cut & Wash

Do you take walk-ins or should I make an appointment?
We accept both walk-ins and appointments at B & H Barber Shop. While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking appointments, especially during peak hours (weekdays 4-7pm and Saturday mornings). You can book by calling us at the shop or through our online booking system. Walk-in wait times typically range from 15-45 minutes depending on the time of day.
How much does a men's haircut cost?
Our standard men's haircut is $35, which includes a consultation, cut, and basic styling. A full-service cut with shampoo and hot towel service is $45. Beard trims are $15 when added to a haircut, or $20 as a standalone service. We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payments like Venmo and CashApp.
How long does a typical haircut appointment take?
A standard men's haircut typically takes 30-35 minutes. If you're adding services like a shampoo, beard trim, or hot towel service, plan for 45-50 minutes. First-time clients should add an extra 5-10 minutes for the initial consultation where we'll discuss your preferred style and any specific concerns.
Are your barbers licensed? What experience do they have?
All our barbers are fully licensed by the New York State Division of Licensing Services and have a minimum of 5 years of professional experience. Our team regularly attends advanced training workshops to stay current with the latest techniques and trends. Each barber specializes in different styles, from classic cuts to modern fades and textured looks.
What if I'm not happy with my haircut?
Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with your cut, please let us know within 48 hours and we'll gladly make adjustments at no additional charge. We recommend booking with the same barber for the adjustment so they can better understand your preferences and ensure you get exactly the style you want.
