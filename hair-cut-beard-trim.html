Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Cut & Beard Trim

Do you take walk-ins or should I book an appointment?
We accept both walk-ins and appointments at B & H Barber Shop. However, we strongly recommend booking an appointment, especially during peak hours (weekday evenings and weekends) to avoid waiting. You can book online through our website or call us at 212-555-0123. Walk-in customers are served on a first-come, first-served basis, and wait times can range from 15-45 minutes during busy periods.
How much do haircuts and beard trims cost?
Our standard men's haircut is $35, which includes a wash and style. Beard trims are $20 when done alone, or $15 when added to a haircut. We also offer a student discount of $5 off with valid ID. Senior cuts (65+) are $30. For first-time clients, we recommend scheduling a consultation with your cut to ensure we understand your style preferences.
How long does a typical appointment take?
A standard men's haircut typically takes 30-35 minutes, including the wash and style. A beard trim usually takes 15-20 minutes. If you're getting both services, plan for about 45 minutes total. For first-time clients, we recommend allowing an extra 5-10 minutes to discuss your preferred style and any specific requirements with your barber.
What's your cancellation policy and how far in advance should I book?
We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may result in a $15 fee charged to the card on file. For booking, we typically recommend scheduling 2-3 days in advance for weekday appointments and 4-5 days ahead for weekend slots. During busy seasons (holidays, back-to-school), booking a week ahead is advised.
Do your barbers have experience with different hair types and styles?
Yes, our team of licensed barbers has extensive experience with all hair types and textures. Each of our barbers has at least 5 years of experience and receives ongoing training in current trends and techniques. We regularly work with straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair textures, and are well-versed in modern styles, classic cuts, fades, and ethnic hair. If you have specific needs, let us know when booking and we can match you with the most experienced barber for your hair type.
