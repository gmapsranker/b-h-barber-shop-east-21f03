Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Cut & Shave

How much does a men's haircut cost and what's included?
Our standard men's haircut is $35, which includes a consultation, shampoo, precision cut, and style. For $50, you can get our signature service that adds a hot towel treatment and neck shave. Senior cuts (65+) are $25. All services include styling with premium products and our barbers take time to understand exactly what style you're looking for.
Do you take walk-ins or should I make an appointment?
We accept walk-ins, but we recommend booking appointments, especially during peak hours (weekday evenings and weekends). You can book online through our website or call us at (212) 555-0123. Walk-in wait times typically range from 15-45 minutes depending on the day and time. We'll always give you an accurate wait time estimate when you come in.
What's your straight razor shave process and how long does it take?
Our traditional straight razor shave ($40) takes about 30-35 minutes. The process includes hot towel preparation, pre-shave oil application, two passes with a straight razor, and a cold towel finish with aftershave balm. We use traditional techniques combined with modern hygiene standards - each client gets a fresh, sterile blade and we're fully licensed by the NY State Board of Barbers.
Do your barbers have experience with ethnic hair types?
Yes, our team of barbers is extensively trained in cutting and styling all hair types and textures. We have particular expertise with African American, Latino, and Asian hair textures. Each of our barbers has at least 5 years of experience and regularly takes continuing education courses to stay current with diverse styling techniques.
What if I'm not happy with my haircut?
We stand behind our work 100%. If you're not completely satisfied with your cut, please let us know within 7 days and we'll fix it free of charge. We want you to love your hair - our East Village reputation depends on it! Just come back in and ask for the same barber (or request a different one if you prefer), and we'll make it right.
