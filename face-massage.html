Frequently Asked Questions About Face Massage

How much does a face massage service cost at B & H Barber Shop?
Our face massage services start at $35 for a 15-minute treatment and $55 for a 30-minute session. We also offer a premium package that includes a hot towel treatment and essential oils for $65. If you combine a face massage with a haircut, we offer a $10 discount on the massage service. All prices include standard moisturizer application.
Do I need to book an appointment for a face massage, or can I walk in?
While we accept walk-ins when possible, we strongly recommend booking an appointment for face massage services, especially during peak hours (11am-2pm and after 5pm). You can book online through our website or call us at our East Village location. We typically require at least 24 hours notice for weekend appointments. Walk-in customers may experience wait times of 30-45 minutes during busy periods.
Are your massage therapists licensed in New York State?
Yes, all our massage therapists are licensed by the New York State Education Department and have completed specific facial massage training. Our lead therapist, Mike, has over 8 years of experience and specializes in tension relief for TMJ and sinus pressure. We maintain copies of all current licenses in our shop and they're available for viewing upon request.
What's included in your basic face massage service?
Our basic face massage service includes a gentle cleansing of the face, neck, and shoulder area, followed by a 15-minute targeted massage using our signature unscented oil. We focus on key pressure points around the temples, jaw, and sinuses. The service concludes with a light moisturizer application. For enhanced relaxation, we recommend upgrading to include our hot towel treatment. Please note that facial hair may affect the massage technique used.
I have sensitive skin - what products do you use during the face massage?
We use hypoallergenic, fragrance-free products specifically designed for sensitive skin. Our primary massage oil is jojoba-based and non-comedogenic (won't clog pores). Before your first service, we'll discuss any skin sensitivities or allergies you may have and adjust our products accordingly. We also offer organic options upon request. If you have specific skin concerns, please let us know when booking, and you're welcome to bring your own products if preferred.
